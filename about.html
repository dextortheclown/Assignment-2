Welcome to our Cake Shop, D Caké, where we create mouthwatering sweet treats that will most definitely satisfy your cravings.

Our story

Welcome to D Caké, where every delightful creation is born from a passion for baking and a commitment to crafting moments of joy. Our story began with a simple dream—to bring the aroma of freshly baked treats and the warmth of homemade goodness to our community.



Founded in 2016, our humble cake shop started in a cozy kitchen, where our head baker, Matty Healy, experimented with flavors, textures, and designs, driven by an unwavering love for baking. Each recipe was meticulously crafted, infused with a blend of tradition and innovation, ensuring that every slice offered a taste of happiness.

Our Mission

- Create delectable, high-quality baked goods that evoke joy and delight in every bite.

- Infuse passion, creativity, and dedication into every cake, cupcake, pastry, and dessert we craft.

- Source the finest, freshest ingredients locally when possible to ensure superior taste and quality.

- Continuously innovate and explore new flavors, designs, and techniques to offer a diverse and evolving menu of treats.

- Spread happiness, one sweet treat at a time, and create lasting memories for our customers.
