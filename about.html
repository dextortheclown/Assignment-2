about us

A Symphony of Flavors Inspired by BTS

Welcome to our BTS Café, where every item and food is a tribute to the world of BTS and their global achievements. Here, we mash the love for coffee, music and the magic of BTS into an extraordinary Café experience.

Immerse yourself in a menu that's as dynamic and diverse as BTS's discography. From classic favorites to innovative concoctions inspired by the members themselves, our café offers a unique twist that caters to every ARMY and café lover out there.

Our story

Step into ARMY Haven, a sanctuary where the love for BTS and café culture converge. Our journey began with a shared dream among ARMYs—to create a space that not only celebrates the music and message of BTS but also crafts moments of joy and connection over coffee and treats.



Launched in the heart of 2020, our café is the culmination of countless hours of planning, passion, and creativity, all fueled by our love for BTS. Each item on our menu, from the "Dynamite" Latte to the "Butter" Croissant, tells a story and embodies the spirit of BTS's music and message.

Our Mission

- To offer a unique café experience that resonates with the themes and values of BTS, bringing joy and inspiration to every ARMY and visitor.

- To craft a diverse menu that blends the art of coffee-making with the essence of BTS, offering both classic and innovative choices.

- To source high-quality ingredients and integrate eco-friendly practices, reflecting BTS's advocacy for a better world.

- To foster a community space where fans can connect, share their love for BTS, and create unforgettable memories.

- To continuously innovate and adapt, ensuring that our café remains a vibrant, welcoming haven for all who step through our doors.
