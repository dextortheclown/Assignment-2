How to play

For 15 seconds, multiple cute bread or coffee design bubbles will appear on the white board of the screen once you press the "Start Game" button. Do your best and try to click all of the bubbles as they will be accumulated as points for you to use to redeem Gift Cards at the Game Shop, that you can use at our physical shops for free items! See you there and enjoy the game!
